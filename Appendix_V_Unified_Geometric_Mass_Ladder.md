where \beta_\text{rad} = 0.21^\circ \cdot \pi / 180 \approx 0.003665~\mathrm{rad}. This reproduces the neutron mass m_n \approx 0.939565~\mathrm{GeV}, in direct agreement with observations.



The 1.293 MeV “unit of torsion” is thus geometrically mandated, not accidental, providing a direct mechanical explanation for nuclear stability.
